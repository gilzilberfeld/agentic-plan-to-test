# Prompt: Gov.il Form Discovery

Use this prompt to let the AI explore the government form before writing any tests.

---

```
Open https://govforms.gov.il/mw/forms/NonIdentifiedForm@tehila.gov.il in a browser.
Explore the form and describe:
- What fields are on the page?
- Which fields are required vs optional?
- What types of inputs are there (text, dropdown, checkbox, etc.)?
- Are there any popups, banners, or overlays?
- How does the form behave when you interact with it?
```

---

**What to expect:**
- The AI opens the browser and navigates to the form.
- It identifies all form fields and their types.
- It notes any cookie banners, loading states, or dynamic behaviour.
- This discovery output feeds directly into the test planning step (slide 15).
