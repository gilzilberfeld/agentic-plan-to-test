# Hello World — Step-by-Step Prompts

Use these three prompts in order with your AI assistant (Claude / Copilot with Playwright MCP).

---

## Step 1 — Open the browser

```
Open https://www.saucedemo.com in a browser
```

---

## Step 2 — Log in

```
Log in with username 'standard_user' and password 'secret_sauce'
```

---

## Step 3 — Describe what you see

```
What do you see on the page after login? Describe the elements.
```

---

**What to expect:**
- Step 1: The browser opens and the Swag Labs login page appears.
- Step 2: The AI fills the username/password fields and clicks Login.
- Step 3: The AI describes the products page — a grid of items, a header, a cart icon, etc.
